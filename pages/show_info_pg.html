Rehearsals

  1. - Attendance at ALL cast rehearsals is mandatory. No exceptions, please
  2. - Please have your dancer arrive in street clothing. Do not have them wear their costume to the theatre. This helps to prevent costume “accidents”. Please clearly label all personal items brought to the theatre.
  3. - Dancers will have a designated area for their costumes and changing needs.
  4. - Please do NOT bring extra things from home unless they are requested.
  5. - Dancers may watch while their NOT on stage for Dress rehearsal ONLY
  6. - Dancers will organize and plan out costume needs back stage with their backstage helpers.
  7. - Dancers are responsible for their costumes backstage.
  8. - Parents are invited to watch dress rehearsal from the auditorium or gym.
  9. - Snacks/water are encouraged, but try to avoid huge messy meals backstage.
  10. - D&G Staff values your time and makes every effort to run rehearsals in a timely fashion.
  11. - Absolutely NO jewelry is to be worn at rehearsal or recital unless part of your costume. No gum allowed.

Audience Arrival and Show

  • - Seating: General Admission/First come-first serve.
  • - Tickets are $5 per person
  • - ALL Ticket stubs will be entered into a raffle drawing for one or all of the following: free dance session/summer camp/Dinner/dessert.
  • - Please remember to take ALL of your belongings. Look under your seats

After the Show

  • - Dancers are responsible for cleaning up their costumes and “stage home” areas.
  • - Remember to take ALL costumes/water bottles/make up that belongs to you. Items that are left will be donated or thrown away.

Volunteer Help and Donations Needed: Thank you in advance for donating your time to help make our show run smoothly!
