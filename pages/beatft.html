Beatfeet Dance Team

D&G Studio Dance Team is a Hip Hop dance squad that performs at many of Seattle's community events, festivals and National causes. Including, Seattle Storm Games, Bumpershoot, Heritage Parade, Breath Deep Seattle and BeatWalk.

We are an inclusive, urban, exciting team that supports each other and have FUN in all we do together!

We have also performed at Internationally known, Monsters of Hip Hop. We have taken classes with current industry leading dancers and choreographers at Monsters of Hip-hop, JUMP, Nuvo & The Pulse with NappyTabs, Luther Brown, Tony Testa, Chonique, Kevin Maher, Lissete, Nick Demoura, Willdabeast, Phi (JBWKEEZ)BEATFEET! We have the BEAT on our FEET!
